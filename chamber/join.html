Chamber of Commerce Membership

Membership Levels

Non Profit Membership

This is a free membership we offer to non profit organizations in the area. Because it is free, it does not include any benefits except for access to special events.

Bronze Membership

This is a $50 membership we offer that includes access to special events and training. Training takes place 4 times a year. It is a weekend event with seminars and classes you can choose to sign up for.

Silver Membership

This is a $100 membership we offer that includes access to special events, training (see description above), and advertising. Details of advertisement will be given upon completion of application.

Gold Membership

This is a $150 membership we offer that includes access to special events, training (see description above), advertising, and event discounts. Details of advertisement and discounts will be given upon completion of application.
